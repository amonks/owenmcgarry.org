The Gothic: A track frame built by myself of True Temper double-butted steel. I also made the handlebars, curly maple handlebar droplets, the forks and I did the paint job. I decided early on that, rather than having a traditional head-badge, I would like a figurehead. Caleb Colpitts sculpted and electroformed the Gargoyle, complete with green sapphire eyes and skunk's teeth


Orbea: The first of several bicycles built from free components found in the trash. This example was built for myself, made to be nimble and narrow for commuting in London's heavy traffic.

Boats: A beetle cat and a sharpie, two of the smaller boats that I helped restore during my apprenticeship at Gannon & Benjamin


Motorcycles: Two motorcycles, both currently work in progress.
