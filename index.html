Someone once accused me of being not a real person; rather, an amalgamation of literary allusions and poetic themes.

I'm from Cambridge, Massachusetts, living in Wellington, NZ. I graduated in 2014 with a BFA in industrial design from the Rhode Island School of Design in Providence, Rhode Island and studied product and furniture design at Kingston University in London. I've apprenticed, and later worked for Gannon & Benjamin Marine Railway on Martha's Vineyard, I've also worked in collaboration with NASA on microgravity workshop environments, as a monitor in the metal shop at RISD, and as an industrial and graphic designer for Mr. Steam in New York City. I am currently in charge of the workshop for furniture designer Duncan Sargent. I have over 14,000 hours of workshop experience.
