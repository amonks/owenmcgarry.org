Someone once accused me of being not a real person; rather, an amalgamation of literary allusions and poetic themes.

I have studied industrial design at the Rhode Island School of Design in Providence, Rhode Island and product and furniture design at Kingston University in London. I've apprenticed under Gannon & Benjamin on Marthas Vineyard and have worked as an industrial and graphic designer for Mr. Steam in New York City. I have over 12,000 hours of workshop experience.
